Our Heritage

A Legacy
Born from
Love

From a single lorry to three iconic outlets — our story is written in every scoop.

1974
The Dream Begins
Mr. Shivprasad Shah starts selling ice cream from a small lorry in Nadiad with a simple vision — to bring joy, one scoop at a time.
1985
Love Builds a Home
Overwhelming customer love transforms the mobile lorry into a beloved shop — Satyanarayan finds its first permanent home.
2000s
A Growing Family
The brand expands to three outlets across Nadiad, each carrying the same warmth, tradition, and authentic taste.
Today
50 Years of Happiness
Generations of families continue to make Satyanarayan their destination for memories, celebrations, and pure happiness.
